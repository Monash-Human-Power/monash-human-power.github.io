About Us

Monash Human Power is a student led Engineering team at Monash University. We are building a fully faired human powered vehicle (HPV) to push the limits of human speed and break the current world record of 144km/h set in Battle Mountain, Nevada. Our journey started as an Engineering Final Year Project in 2014 and in 2016 we became Monash Human Power. Since then, we have grown as a team and now have 30 members in a number of diverse roles throughout the team. 

The Bike

Our bike is a specially designed recumbent bicycle housed in a fairing to reduce aerodynamic drag.
 
Each major component of the bike was designed and manufactured to meet a range of criteria and act as a functional part of the bike to help our rider achieve top speed.
